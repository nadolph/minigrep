Happy Birthday  🎂  Official Cook-Off
Welcome to 38 Steaks — a birthday celebration where the real gift is the perfect sear. Competitors bring their best Canadian-raised protein, fire it up, and let the flavors speak for themselves. No frills, no fuss — just flame, meat, and glory.

What Wins the Cook-Off?

Judges score on two things only. Make them count.

👅 Flavor Seasoning, depth, complexity
🍽️ Texture Crust, tenderness, bite

Sides are not judged. Presentation is not judged. Just the steak.

📋 Official Rules

🇨🇦
Protein Canadian-raised beef, pork, game, or any non-meat alternative is permitted.
⚖️
Total Weight Minimum 16 oz prepared. No maximum — go big.
🥩
Portions Multiple portions are allowed, but all must be identical in cut and preparation. Doneness may vary between portions.
📏
Portion Size Each individual portion may not exceed 48 oz.
🍳
Sear Requirement Every portion must be seared on all sides. No exceptions.
📐
Shape & Thickness Slab-shaped: no more than 2 inches thick.
Baseball-shaped: no more than 3 inches thick.
🧂
Crust / Covering Allowed, but must be ≤ ¼ inch thick and cover no more than 4 sides. Exception: a single-side covering may be up to ½ inch thick. May not be made of meat — bacon excepted.
🔍
Insertions / Stuffing Anything inserted into the steak must be smaller than ½ tsp per insertion and may not be made of meat.
🥣
Sauce One sauce on the side is permitted. Keep it saucy, not showy.
🔪
Slicing for Tasting Steaks must be sliced into bite-size pieces before being presented to judges.
